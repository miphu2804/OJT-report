### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is open and easy to adapt to. People at FCJ are very supportive whenever I run into issues, even beyond working hours. The workspace is organized and comfortable, which helps me stay focused. If possible, adding more team bonding activities would make collaboration even stronger.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provides clear and practical guidance, and always encourages questions when something is unclear. The admin team is also responsive with procedures, documents, and operational support. What I appreciate most is that my mentor lets me attempt solutions first instead of immediately giving the final answer.

**3. Networking Culture & Professional Connections**  
I highly value the team's networking culture, especially proactive professional connection building through LinkedIn and peer exchanges. This helped me expand my industry network and learn practical perspectives from more experienced members.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I noticeably improved my teamwork and collaboration in real project settings. I also strengthened my ability to plan work and use multiple tools to track progress, which made my workflow more structured and effective.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is positive and respectful. People work seriously but still maintain a comfortable and collaborative atmosphere. During urgent projects, everyone supports each other regardless of role, which made me feel included as a true member of the team, not just an intern.

### Additional Questions

- **Question:** What did I find most satisfying during my internship?  
	**Answer:** What I appreciated most was being trusted with real tasks while still receiving close guidance from my mentor and team. Each task had a clear goal, and I had room to research and think before review, so I could clearly see my progress week by week.

- **Question:** What could the company improve for future interns?  
	**Answer:** I think the company could provide a more structured onboarding plan for the first one or two weeks, such as a reading checklist, recommended foundational topics, and short orientation sessions by domain. This would help new interns adapt faster and reduce early confusion.

- **Question:** Would I recommend this internship to my friends? Why?  
	**Answer:** Yes, I would. The environment is supportive, mentors are knowledgeable and approachable, and the tasks are challenging enough to build real-world skills. It is a strong place for students who want serious growth in Cloud/DevOps and professional working habits.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

- **Question:** Do I have suggestions to improve the internship experience?  
	**Answer:**
	- Add more short internal knowledge-sharing sessions on practical topics (CI/CD, observability, incident handling).  
	- Maintain a centralized intern knowledge base (guidelines, report templates, best practices) for easier self-learning.  
	- Organize more cross-team networking activities so interns can learn from different perspectives.

- **Question:** Would I like to continue this program in the future?  
	**Answer:** Yes. If there is an opportunity, I would like to continue in a more advanced role to deepen my technical skills and contribute more to the team.

- **Question:** Any other comments?  
	**Answer:** I sincerely appreciate the mentor and team for their patience and support throughout the internship. I hope the program continues to grow so that more students can benefit from this kind of high-quality learning and working environment.
