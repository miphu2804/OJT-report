#### Tài nguyên được tạo (theo thứ tự)

1. **Networking**: VPC, subnets, route tables, Internet Gateway.
2. **Security**: security group, IAM roles/policies.
3. **Compute**: ALB, EC2 Auto Scaling, target group.
4. **Storage/Registry**: S3 buckets, ECR repository.
5. **Database/Cache**: RDS/DocDB/Redis (nếu có).
6. **Monitoring**: log group, alarm, SNS (nếu bật).
